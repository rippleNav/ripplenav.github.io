Let's begin with a familiar example that will help us understand the process.

Imagine you're playing a simple guessing game with another agent. They've chosen a secret number between 1 and 100, and you want to find it as quickly as possible. Now, you could start guessing random numbers, 17, 42, 83, but that might take a very long time.

  5. You continue this dance of questions, "Is it greater than 87?", each time dividing the remaining range in half until you zero in on the exact number.
