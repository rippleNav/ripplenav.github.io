Advanced Methods

The distance method:

In the previous module, we learned how to focus our attention on people and beings to identify and resolve our UTE's.

This approach is effective, but it assumes that you can readily identify a being in your mental space. However, what happens when you're dealing with an emotional issue or struggling to achieve a goal, yet you're unable to pinpoint the specific being associated with an UTE?

To overcome this challenge, a more advanced technique known as "The Distance Method" is introduced. This method is particularly useful for discovering beings whose memories you have inadvertently absorbed, by focusing on spatial awareness.

22

While engaging with the  RippleNav,  mentally pose the question: "The being whose memories are influencing my current problem, where are you located?" Consider the following directions:

  1. In front of me?
  2. To my right?
  3. To my left?
  4. Behind me?
  5. Above me?
  6. Below me?

 "source" 

If you receive varying responses, focus on the direction that elicits the strongest reaction as your starting point.

Next, determine the precise distance in that direction, such as 5.23 meters. Utilize the  RippleNav  to ascertain this distance, starting from a baseline of 5 meters and adjusting using 'greater than' or 'less than' as needed. At this stage, you should find this process straightforward.

Distances typically range from 1 to 20 meters, though some may be as close as 0.1 millimeters or as far as several kilometers. It's crucial not to dwell on the significance of these distances; they are merely directional markers and numerical addresses for the beings in 'mind space', and do not correlate to physical distances. For example, a being located 2.12 meters above you is not literally in the ceiling, but at that distance in your mental perception.

Once you have determined the being's 'address', note it down. You can then apply the techniques from ealier to locate and address UTE's.

Alternatively, you can identify a 'problem area' using a long list of potential problems, and then employ the distance method to find the being responsible. Once located, proceed as before.

You'll soon realize that you are connected to billions of beings in this manner. Addressing these connections is a considerable task, and it's recommended to dedicate around 2 hours daily to this practice.

To help you find more UTE's you can also go onto Youtube and watch videos on topics you DO NOT like. Things that disturb you and make you feel uncomfortable usually stem from UTE's. Any thought that produces a right-movement on the RippleNav is a UTE and can be addressed using the above technique.

Make a list of such things which disturb and upset you and then work through the list systimatically. When you think you have reached a point where your current list is no longer producing results. Wait a few weeks then come back to that list and it will produce new results. Meanwhile, make a new list.

You are connected to billions of beings in this manner and all of those UTE's are negatively impacting you in some way. If you think you can't find any UTE's then you are simply not trying hard enough or not looking in or avoiding potential areas. Get out of your comfort zone, that's where the problems are.
