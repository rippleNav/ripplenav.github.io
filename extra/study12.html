Locate the Source

Being: A being is any living creature, from a person to a bug. Being also refers to the state of existing. "To be, or not to be" , that is the question when you're talking about being. Things that exist are in a state of being: this meaning of being is a little vague, but it has to do with the way things are alive and real.

Up until now you have been removing mental blocks ( UTE's 'merges' ) by either focusing on an unwanted emotion or by using a list of problems to find targets.

You can make a list of things which disturb and upset you and then work through the list systimatically. When you think you have reached a point where your current list is no longer producing results. Wait a few weeks then come back to that list and it will produce new results. Meanwhile, make a new list.

Now we want you to focus on other people directly. This is actually the prefered method of removing 'merges' and you should use the below method from now on. You may still use lists but use this new method. This can get quite active so be prepared.

To clarify, the difference between this method and the previous is that here you locate a person or being in your mental space, THEN remove your 'merge' incidents from that person. The previous method had you locate a merge incident directly and then undo it, which can leave some stirred up energy unhandled. Always use this new method from now on.

Here you would, while watching the  RippleNav,  focus your attention on a particular person or being. Trees, animals, pets etc are beings with which you may likely have UTE's with. It's not just about people, but most people would begin this section by seeking out other people.

If you get a read from the  RippleNav  while putting your attention on a particular person then you do have UTE's with that person or being. You can also verify this by mentally asking the person or being "Do we have any UTE's?"

Once you have located a suitable person or being, you do as you did before, except that now the person or being is the target of your attention.

If the concept of a "being" causes discomfort, it's worth remembering that you, too, are a being. The essence of life in any living entity stems from a being that interacts with and governs it. When life ends, the being itself does not perish; rather, it departs, potentially embarking on a new existence elsewhere. Embrace this perspective to alleviate any fears regarding other beings; they are fundamentally similar to you. This universal truth extends to everyone, including those you cherish and even those you may dislike. Each one is a being, just like you.

So let's continue.

Don't worry, it's still those same seven steps as before, except that before step 1, you find a being in your mental space that provides a strong response on the  RippleNav.  Once you have a suitable being, you use the seven steps to remove your UTE's from that being.

Additional Information

Seeing as you have now had several hours of experience with the  RippleNav,  you may also have noticed that the device sometimes twitches as if it's trying to get your attention or reprimand you for something. Well, all of that is true. You have been interacting with beings since the beginning and they will try and tell you things like "NO! the number is MUCH higher" or "HEY! get your attention back here and carry on" etc.

Understanding why a "being" can't simply identify and address the very first UTE can be perplexing. Here's a simplified explanation: Time and space are concepts that exist within a universe, not outside of it. Considering the multitude of universes that exist, most, if not all, could contain UTE's.

When a being attempts to locate the first UTE, they typically find the earliest one within this universe. While this is a significant discovery, it's not the complete picture. If you refer to the  RippleNav,  you'll notice there are additional UTE's with that being. These likely reside in other universes. But pinpointing the first universe a being existed in is complex because time doesn't function between universes in the way we understand it.

It's a challenging concept to grasp and may require some reflection. Essentially, a being cannot directly access the first-ever UTE shared with you because there are multiple 'first' incidents across different universes, not just one. This complexity is a key aspect of understanding the nature of beings and their interactions across universes.

If you look around in your mind you will find a great many "beings" with whom you have UTE's. Many of those incidents were quite traumatic at the time and can provide great relief when the incident is undone. It's the trapped emotions in those incidents that cause all the anxiety and other emotional problems which people have.
