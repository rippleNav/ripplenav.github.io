Before you begin

Agent, this is the final preparation before you begin working directly with the  RippleNav  tool to make you mission ready

Everything we've explored so far has been laying the foundation for this moment. The instructions I'm about to share will also appear on the page where you'll use the actual  RippleNav , so don't worry about memorizing everything right away. With practice, these steps will become second nature.

Let's walk through each step of the process together. If you're new to this work, remember to begin with the "Brain Stretch" exercise we explored in Module 9, this will help you expand your consciousness beyond your everyday awareness.

All of the following guidance is meant to be followed silently, in the quiet space of your mind. There's no need to speak aloud, the  RippleNav  responds to your conscious intention, not to your voice.

There's also something important I haven't mentioned before: the  RippleNav  is meant for your eyes only during a session. Please don't invite someone else to watch while you're using it. The tool is likely to respond to both of your energetic fields simultaneously, creating patterns of movement that won't make sense to either of you. Think of it as a private conversation between you and your deeper consciousness.

You might be wondering how this tool can address mental health challenges like anxiety, depression, or specific phobias. These issues connect to you through magic numbers, much like the way a specific frequency can tune into a particular radio station. To work with any mental health concern, you'll focus on a feeling or urge associated with it and find the magic number that links to that experience.

If this seems a bit abstract right now, please don't worry. Until this point, we've been exploring theory without practice. Soon you'll have hands-on experience with the tool, and everything will begin to make much more sense.

I know that using this tool for the first time might feel intimidating. Many people find it helpful to print out the steps or take notes to reference during their first few sessions. I recommend reading through the instructions several times, then trying to recall them from memory.

You'll likely make some mistakes at first, and that's perfectly okay. It's part of the learning process. Think about when you first learned to drive a car. At first, you had to consciously think about every action, turning the key, checking mirrors, applying the right pressure to the pedals. But with practice, these actions became automatic. The same will happen with the  RippleNav .

Remember that you're engaging in this healing work to improve yourself and your life, not as some form of punishment or obligation. Approach it with gentle curiosity rather than harsh judgment.

The Healing Process

As a reminder, we use the term UTE for "Unifying Traumatic Experience", the merge incidents we've discussed in previous modules. The fundamental process for finding and releasing these merged experiences is beautifully simple:

There are just 2 steps shown below.
  1. Find the 'UTE'
  2. Find the magic number

However, to ensure you're approaching this process with awareness and precision, we are going to expand these two steps into a seven-step protocol that includes important verification checks.

There is no need to get flustered. We are still dealing with the above TWO steps, but now we just check it more thoroughly.

Here are the seven steps we'll follow:

  1. Find the 1st 'UTE'
  2. Make sure you found it
  3. Find the magic number
  4. Did the 'merge' dissolve?
  5. If separation did not occur, go to 3
  6. Make SURE it dissolved
  7. Are all the merges dissolved for this problem?

Step 1: Find the First UTE

Let's say you want to address a specific challenge, perhaps feelings of sadness, persistent depression, a phobia of spiders, or any other mental or emotional pattern that's disrupting your wellbeing.

Begin by mentally addressing the universe while watching the  RippleNav . In the silence of your mind, say, "Find the first UTE which links me to..." and then name your specific concern.

This particular sequence of merged experiences becomes your "Target", the focus of your healing work.

Step 2: Make Sure You Found It

After stating your intention, ask, "Did you find it?" The  RippleNav  should respond with a brief movement to the right. If you don't see this confirmation, simply repeat the previous step until the  RippleNav  indicates it has located the UTE.

If you find that you're not getting any response at all, there are two possible explanations. Either the issue you're addressing isn't connected to a merge experience (which is rare), or, more likely, you're approaching it too broadly.

For example, instead of focusing on "feeling depressed" as a general state, you might need to be more specific, such as "feeling depressed about my relationship" or "feeling depressed about my career path." The more precise you can be, the more effectively the  RippleNav  can locate the corresponding UTE.

Step 3: Find the Magic Number

Using the halving method we learned in Module 7, find the magic number for the UTE you've just located. As you search, keep your Target from step 1 clearly in your awareness.

We typically begin our search at 5,000 and proceed from there, checking "greater than" or "less than" to narrow down the possibilities. When you've discovered the number, mentally tell your Target, "The number is..." and state the number you've found.

Step 4: Did the Merge Dissolve?

Always keep your attention on the  RippleNav . Within 1 to 5 seconds after telling your Target what the magic number is, you should observe a longer "release" movement.

A release occurs when a merge dissolves, freeing you from the unconscious memories that weren't authentically yours. This release is often accompanied by a subtle sensation of tension leaving your body or mind.

Along with an actual 'feeling' of relief, there are three distinct patterns that indicate a release, all lasting between 1 and several seconds:

  1. A long, sustained movement to the right
  2. A quick movement to the right followed by a longer movement to the left
  3. An erratic, dance-like movement that goes in both left and right directions

If you don't observe any of these release patterns, it likely means you haven't found the correct magic number. This happens sometimes, it's part of the process.

Step 5: Make SURE It Dissolved

To verify that the merge has truly dissolved, ask your Target, "Did it dissolve?" or "Did it separate?", whichever terminology resonates more with you.

If you don't receive a response, try asking the opposite: "Did it not dissolve?" or "Did it not separate?"

These verification questions help ensure that you're accurately interpreting the responses from the  RippleNav .

Step 6: If Separation Did Not Occur

If the  RippleNav  indicates that separation did not occur, meaning the merged memories haven't been released, then your magic number isn't quite right. You might be off by a small amount, perhaps a few hundred or thousand, or you could be significantly off target.

In this case, return to Step 3 and try working with very different numbers. You might start with 500, or perhaps 5 million, or even 5 quadrillion, and proceed from there. Trust your intuition to guide you toward the right scale.

Step 7: Are All the Merges Dissolved for This Problem?

Once you've successfully released a merge, it's possible that you've reached the first UTE in the sequence. This is typically accompanied by a more profound sense of wellbeing or release than you've experienced with previous numbers.

To check whether you've completed the entire sequence, you can ask your Target questions like:

  1. "Are we still connected?"
  2. "Gone?"
  3. "Went away?"

If the Target is completely released, then asking "Are we still connected?" should receive no response, while questions like "Gone?" or "Went away?" should elicit a confirmation.

If you haven't yet reached the first UTE in the sequence, simply return to Step 1 and repeat the process until you've fully resolved the issue.

It's important to note that even though you always ask for the "first UTE," you will rarely find it on your first attempt. This is why you need to repeat the steps until you reach that initial merge point. The reason for this involves some complex aspects of consciousness that we won't explore just yet, but trust that the process works when followed with patience and presence.

The process we've just explored allows you to identify these merges and release them, gradually restoring your true energetic signature and authentic emotional landscape.

I honor the courage and commitment you're bringing to this healing journey. Remember that transformation isn't always linear, there may be moments of profound insight followed by periods of integration. Trust the process and trust your inner wisdom as you move forward.
