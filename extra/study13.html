In prior modules you were broadly looking for and undoing UTE's affecting you. As you make progress, you may find that you need to narow yor focus to specific area's of interest. You may be grappling with issues that are not immediately apparent to you. While your previous approach has been effective in addressing problems you're consciously aware of, it's important to remember that the unconscious mind operates largely out of sight. This means that the root causes of your 'known' issues might be different from what you perceive. In fact, they could be deeply buried in your subconscious, potentially unrelated to what you initially believe. Understanding and addressing these hidden aspects is key to a more comprehensive self-awareness and resolution of these issues.

To help you narrow down your focus to specific areas of your life which may contain hidden trauma, we have devised this category tree to aid you. Observe the  RippleNav,  while moving your attention through each of the categories. If you see some movement to the right then you know that the category has something to dig into. Use the  RippleNav,  to locate the being who is 'energising' that category or subcategory and then proceed as before to remove all your UTE's with that being.
